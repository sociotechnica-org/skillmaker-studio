# Design — Changelog Entry Writer

## Intent

When a repo maintainer finishes a change (a commit or a small set of
commits), they need a well-formed entry appended to `CHANGELOG.md` under
the "Unreleased" heading, summarizing the change for someone who will
later read the changelog to decide whether to upgrade. The skill should
save the maintainer from writing that summary by hand every time.
